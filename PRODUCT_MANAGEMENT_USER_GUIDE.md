<h4 style="color: #374151;">How to Select Products:</h4>

1. **View Your Products**
   - When you open Product Management, you'll see a list of all your products
   - Each product shows:
     - Product image (small thumbnail)
     - Product name
     - Current stock level
     - Number of variants (if it has sizes/colors/etc.)

2. **Filter Products** (Optional - makes finding products easier!)
   
   **By Stock Level:**
   - Click one of these buttons at the top:
     - **All Products** - Shows everything
     - **Out of Stock** - Only shows products with 0 inventory
     - **Critical** - Shows products with 1-5 items left (running low!)
     - **Low Stock** - Shows products with 6-20 items
     - **In Stock** - Shows products with 21+ items
   
   **By Search:**
   - Type in the search box to find specific products by name
   - Example: Type "shirt" to see all products with "shirt" in the name

3. **Select Products** (3 ways to do this!)
   
   **Option A: Select Individual Products**
   - Click the checkbox next to each product you want to edit
   - Selected products will have a checkmark ‚úì
   
   **Option B: Select All Products**
   - Click the "Select All" checkbox at the very top
   - This selects EVERY product on your current page
   
   **Option C: Select All Filtered Products**
   - First, use filters to narrow down (example: filter by "Out of Stock")
   - Then click "Select All"
   - Now only out-of-stock products are selected!

4. **See What You Selected**
   - At the top, you'll see a summary: "X products selected (Y variants)"
   - Variants = different versions (like size Small, Medium, Large)
   - Below that, you'll see small thumbnails of your selected products

5. **Ready? Click "Next Step"**
   - The blue "Next Step" button at the bottom
   - This takes you to STEP 2 where you actually edit things!

---

Now you'll see **5 big buttons** - these are your editing options:

- üí∞ **Pricing** - Change prices

- ‚úèÔ∏è **Content** - Change titles and descriptions

## üí∞ PRICING - How to Change Prices

### What Can You Do?
- **Increase prices** by a percentage (example: raise all prices by 10%)
- **Decrease prices** by a percentage (example: discount everything by 15%)
- **Round prices** to neat numbers (example: $19.99 becomes $20.00)
- **Set compare-at prices** (the "was $50, now $40" strikethrough price)
- **Update cost per item** (what YOU paid for it - for profit tracking)

### Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. **After clicking "Pricing", you'll see options. Choose one:**

   **A. Increase Price by Percentage**
   - Click the "Increase" button
   - Enter a number in the "Percentage" box (example: type `10` for 10% increase)
   - Example: If a product costs $20, a 10% increase makes it $22
   - Click "Apply Price Changes" button

   **B. Decrease Price by Percentage**
   - Click the "Decrease" button  
   - Enter a number in the "Percentage" box (example: type `15` for 15% off)
   - Example: If a product costs $20, a 15% decrease makes it $17
   - Click "Apply Price Changes" button

   **C. Round Prices**
   - Click the "Round" button
   - Choose how to round:
     - **Up** - $19.50 becomes $20.00
     - **Down** - $19.50 becomes $19.00
     - **Nearest** - $19.50 becomes $20.00, but $19.40 becomes $19.00
   - Choose what to round to:
     - **Dollar** - Makes prices like $20.00 (no cents)
     - **50 cents** - Makes prices end in .00 or .50 ($19.50, $20.00, $20.50)
     - **99 cents** - Makes prices end in .99 ($19.99, $20.99)
   - Check the box "Also round compare-at prices" if you want to round the strikethrough prices too
   - Click "Apply Price Changes" button

2. **Add a "Was/Now" Sale Price (Optional)**
   - Find the "Set Compare At Price" section
   - Check the box "Set compare-at price as current price"
   - This makes the current price show as "was $X" with a line through it
   - The new lower price you set will show as the sale price!

3. **Update What You Paid (Cost Per Item) - Optional**
   - Find "Cost Per Item" section
   - Check the box "Update cost per item"
   - Choose "Set to" and enter the cost (example: $10)
   - This helps you track profit (you paid $10, selling for $20 = $10 profit)
   - This is only for YOUR records - customers don't see this!

4. **Set Taxable (Optional)**
   - Check "Mark as taxable" if this product should charge sales tax
   - Uncheck if it shouldn't charge tax

5. **Click "Apply Price Changes"**
   - You'll see a green success message: "‚úÖ Successfully updated X products!"
   - Done! Your prices are changed!

## üìÅ COLLECTIONS - Add or Remove from Collections

### What Can You Do?
- **Add products** to one or more collections
- **Remove products** from collections

### Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. **After clicking "Collections", you'll see a dropdown menu**
   - Click on it to see ALL your store's collections
   - Example collections: "Summer Sale", "New Arrivals", "Best Sellers", etc.

2. **Select Collection(s)**
   - Click on a collection name to select it
   - You can select MULTIPLE collections!
   - Selected collections show with a checkmark

3. **Choose an Action:**

   **A. Add to Collections**
   - Click the "Add to Collections" button
   - Your selected products will be added to those collections
   - Example: Select "Summer Sale" ‚Üí Click "Add" ‚Üí All your products now appear in Summer Sale!

   **B. Remove from Collections**
   - Click the "Remove from Collections" button
   - Your selected products will be removed from those collections
   - Example: Season ended? Remove all products from "Summer Sale"

4. **Click the button and wait**
   - You'll see: "‚úÖ Successfully added X products to Y collections!"
   - Or: "‚úÖ Successfully removed X products from Y collections!"
   - Done! Check your collections to see the changes!

## üè∑Ô∏è TAGS - Add, Remove, or Replace Tags

### What Can You Do?
- **Add new tags** to products (keeps existing tags)
- **Remove specific tags** from products
- **Replace ALL tags** with new ones

### What Are Tags?
Tags are keywords that help you organize products. Examples:
- "new"
- "sale"  
- "bestseller"
- "winter"
- "featured"

### Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. **After clicking "Tags", choose what you want to do:**

   **A. Add Tags (Keeps Existing Tags)**
   - Click the "Add" button
   - In the text box, type your tags
   - **Separate multiple tags with commas**
   - Example: `new, featured, bestseller`
   - This ADDS these tags without removing old ones
   - Click "Update Product Tags" button

   **B. Remove Tags**
   - Click the "Remove" button
   - Type the tags you want to DELETE
   - **Separate with commas**
   - Example: `old, discontinued`
   - This removes only those specific tags (keeps all others)
   - Click "Update Product Tags" button

   **C. Replace ALL Tags**
   - Click the "Replace" button
   - Type your NEW tags
   - Example: `new, spring, 2025`
   - **Warning**: This DELETES all old tags and replaces with these new ones!
   - Click "Update Product Tags" button

2. **Wait for Success Message**
   - You'll see: "‚úÖ Successfully updated tags for X products!"
   - Done! Your tags are updated!

## ‚úèÔ∏è CONTENT - Edit Titles and Descriptions

### What Can You Do?
- **Modify product titles** (the name customers see)
- **Modify product descriptions** (the detailed text about the product)

---

### **Part 1: TITLES**

Click the "Titles" tab at the top.

#### Options for Titles:

**A. Add Prefix (Add Text to Beginning)**
- Click "Add Prefix" button
- Type the text you want at the START of every title
- Example: Type `üî• HOT DEAL - ` 
- Result: "T-Shirt" becomes "üî• HOT DEAL - T-Shirt"
- Click "Update Product Titles" button

**B. Add Suffix (Add Text to End)**
- Click "Add Suffix" button
- Type the text you want at the END of every title
- Example: Type ` - LIMITED TIME`
- Result: "T-Shirt" becomes "T-Shirt - LIMITED TIME"
- Click "Update Product Titles" button

**C. Find & Replace**
- Click "Find & Replace" button
- **Find Text box**: Type the OLD text you want to find
  - Example: `Shirt`
- **Replace With box**: Type the NEW text
  - Example: `Premium Shirt`
- Result: "Blue Shirt" becomes "Blue Premium Shirt"
- **Leave "Replace With" empty to DELETE the found text!**
- Click "Update Product Titles" button

---

### **Part 2: DESCRIPTIONS**

Click the "Descriptions" tab at the top.

#### Options for Descriptions:

**A. Set All (Replace Entire Description)**
- Click "Set All" button
- Type the NEW description you want for ALL products
- **Warning**: This completely replaces all existing descriptions!
- You can use HTML for formatting:
  - `<b>Bold text</b>`
  - `<br/>` for line breaks
  - `<ul><li>Bullet points</li></ul>`
- Example: `<b>High Quality Product</b><br/>Made with care.`
- Click "Update Product Descriptions" button

**B. Add Prefix (Add Text to Beginning)**
- Click "Add Prefix" button
- Type text you want BEFORE existing descriptions
- Example: `‚ö†Ô∏è SALE ITEM - `
- Result: Adds this to the start, keeps the rest of the description
- Click "Update Product Descriptions" button

**C. Add Suffix (Add Text to End)**
- Click "Add Suffix" button
- Type text you want AFTER existing descriptions
- Example: `<br/><b>Free Shipping!</b>`
- Result: Adds this to the end, keeps the rest of the description
- Click "Update Product Descriptions" button

**D. Find & Replace**
- Click "Find & Replace" button
- **Find Text**: Type text to search for in descriptions
- **Replace With**: Type the new text (or leave empty to delete)
- Example: Replace all mentions of "2024" with "2025"
- Click "Update Product Descriptions" button

## üì¶ INVENTORY - Update Stock Levels

### What Can You Do?
- **Set exact stock numbers** (example: set all to 100)
- **Add to current stock** (example: add 50 to whatever is there now)
- **Remove from current stock** (example: reduce all by 10)
- **Update SKU** (your internal product code)
- **Update Barcode** (the scannable barcode number)
- **Allow selling when out of stock**

### Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. **After clicking "Inventory", you'll see:**
   - A summary showing how many variants you're updating
   - Total current inventory across all selected products

2. **Choose How to Update Stock:**

   **A. Set to Exact Number**
   - Click the "Method" dropdown
   - Select "Set to"
   - Enter a number (example: `100`)
   - Result: EVERY variant will have exactly 100 in stock
   - Ignores what was there before!

   **B. Add to Existing Stock**
   - Select "Add"
   - Enter a number (example: `50`)
   - Result: If you had 20 in stock, now you have 70 (20 + 50)

   **C. Remove from Existing Stock**
   - Select "Remove"
   - Enter a number (example: `10`)
   - Result: If you had 20 in stock, now you have 10 (20 - 10)

3. **Update SKU (Optional)**
   - SKU = Stock Keeping Unit (your product code)
   - Check the box "Update SKU"
   - Enter the SKU (example: `SHIRT-BLU-001`)
   - This sets the SAME SKU for all selected variants

4. **Update Barcode (Optional)**
   - Check the box "Update Barcode"  
   - Enter the barcode number (example: `123456789012`)
   - This sets the SAME barcode for all selected variants

5. **Continue Selling When Out of Stock (Optional)**
   - Check "Allow customers to purchase when out of stock"
   - This lets customers buy even when you show "0 in stock"
   - Useful for pre-orders or made-to-order items!

6. **Click "Update Product Inventory"**
   - You'll see: "‚úÖ Successfully updated inventory for X variants!"
   - Done! Your stock levels are updated!

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Important Tips & Warnings

### ‚úã Before You Click "Apply" or "Update":

1. **DOUBLE-CHECK YOUR SELECTION**
   - Look at the thumbnails of selected products
   - Make sure you selected the RIGHT products!
   - Use filters to narrow down first if needed

2. **PRICE CHANGES ARE IMMEDIATE**
   - Once you click "Apply Price Changes", it happens instantly
   - There's no undo button!
   - Start with a small test if you're unsure

3. **"REPLACE" MEANS DELETE OLD STUFF**
   - In Tags: "Replace" deletes ALL old tags
   - In Titles/Descriptions: "Set All" or "Replace" removes old content
   - Use "Add Prefix/Suffix" if you want to keep existing text!

4. **VARIANTS = MULTIPLE UPDATES**
   - If a product has 5 sizes (variants), you're updating all 5
   - The variant count tells you how many individual items you're changing

5. **INVENTORY METHOD MATTERS**
   - "Set to" = Exact number (ignores current stock)
   - "Add" = Adds to current stock
   - "Remove" = Subtracts from current stock
   - Choose carefully!

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### "I don't see any products!"
- Check your filters - you might be filtering out everything
- Click "All Products" to reset filters
- Try clicking "Select All" to see if products appear

### "The operation didn't work!"
- Make sure you clicked the final "Apply" or "Update" button
- Check for error messages at the top (red notifications)
- Verify you selected products in Step 1
- Make sure you filled in all required fields (marked with *)

### "I changed the wrong products!"
- Go to each product manually to fix
- Or: Select those products again and update them back
- Unfortunately, there's no undo button - be careful!

### "My percentage calculation seems wrong"
- Example: 10% increase on $20 = $22 (20 √ó 1.10)
- Example: 15% decrease on $20 = $17 (20 √ó 0.85)
- Check your math or try a small test first!
